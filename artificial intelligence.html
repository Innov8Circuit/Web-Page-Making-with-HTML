Artificial Intelligence Expert Roadmap

Step 1: Learn the Basics

  1. Mathematics:
    • Linear Algebra: Khan Academy
    • Calculus: MIT OpenCourseWare
    • Probability and Statistics: Coursera or edX
  2. Programming:
    • Python: Codecademy, Coursera, or Udemy
    • Data Structures and Algorithms: HackerRank, LeetCode

Step 2: Introduction to AI and Machine Learning

  • "Machine Learning" by Andrew Ng (Coursera)
  • "Introduction to TensorFlow for Artificial Intelligence" (Coursera)
  • "Deep Learning Specialization" by Andrew Ng (Coursera)

Step 3: Dive Deeper into Specializations

  1. Deep Learning:
    • Books: "Deep Learning" by Ian Goodfellow
    • Courses: "Deep Learning Specialization" by Andrew Ng (Coursera)
  2. Natural Language Processing:
    • Courses: "Natural Language Processing with Classification and Vector Spaces" (Coursera)
    • Books: "Speech and Language Processing" by Daniel Jurafsky and James H. Martin
  3. Computer Vision:
    • Courses: "Convolutional Neural Networks" by Andrew Ng (Coursera)
    • Books: "Deep Learning for Computer Vision" by Adrian Rosebrock

Step 4: Practical Experience and Projects

  • Start with simple projects like digit recognition using MNIST dataset.
  • Gradually move to more complex projects like image classification, sentiment analysis, and reinforcement learning.

Step 5: Advanced Topics and Deployment

  1. Advanced Topics:
    • Reinforcement Learning: "Deep Reinforcement Learning" (Udacity)
    • Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs): Online tutorials and research papers
  2. Deployment:
    • Learn to deploy models using Flask or FastAPI.
    • Explore cloud services for AI model deployment (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure).

Step 6: Continuous Learning and Networking

  • Follow AI researchers on Twitter.
  • Read the latest research papers on arXiv.
  • Attend AI conferences and workshops.
  • Join AI communities and forums.
  • Collaborate with peers and contribute to open-source projects.
